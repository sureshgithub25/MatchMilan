MatchMate is a sleek and simple app that displays match profiles in a card-style layout, inspired by popular matrimonial platforms.
You can easily swipe through profiles, accept or decline matches, and your preferences are saved and stored locally on your device.

Key Features

Beautiful match cards with profile images, names, and locations.

Accept or decline a match with one tap.

Choices are persistently stored using CoreData.

All profiles and your selections are cached for offline access.
